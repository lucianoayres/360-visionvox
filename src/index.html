This application enhances your viewing experience with YouTube 360-degree videos, using AI to enable voice commands and head movements for seamless control.

Enable your browser's microphone and camera to begin.

Voice Commands

Control Video Playback with Voice:

  • Say "PLAY" to start the video.
  • Say "PAUSE" to pause the video.
  • Say "STOP" to stop the video.
  • Say "NEXT" to play the next video.
  • Say "BACK" to play the previous video.

Head Movement Controls

Control 360 Video with Head Movements:

  • Look Right: Move the video to the right.
  • Look Left: Move the video to the left.
  • Look Up: Move the video up.
  • Look Down: Move the video down.
  • Look Center: Stop movement.
